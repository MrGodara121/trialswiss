Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 13, 2026 | Version: 1.2

1. What Data We Collect

We collect only the following personal information:

  • Name: For identification
  • Email: For communication and login
  • Company: For business context
  • API usage data: For service optimization

We do NOT collect:

  • Credit card information (handled by PayPal)
  • Passwords (we use API keys instead)
  • Health information
  • Location data beyond country

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for:

  • Providing access to our platform
  • Sending service-related emails
  • Improving our services
  • Billing and account management

We do NOT sell your data to anyone.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Sheets with Google's enterprise-grade security. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. We implement API key validation for all access.

4. Your GDPR Rights

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access your data - Request a copy
  • Rectify your data - Correct errors
  • Delete your data - Right to be forgotten
  • Export your data - Data portability

Email privacy@trialswiss.com for any requests.

5. Swiss FADP Compliance

We comply with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP). Our data processing is in accordance with Swiss law.

6. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for functionality. No tracking cookies without consent.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, data is removed within 30 days.
