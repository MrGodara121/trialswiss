Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP) Compliance

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Compliance Statement

TrialsWiss AG complies fully with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP, nFADP) which came into force on September 1, 2023.

Data Processing in Switzerland

All data processing activities are conducted in accordance with Swiss law. Our servers and data storage are located within Switzerland and the European Economic Area.

Your Rights Under Swiss Law

  • Right to information about your data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
